The game's objective is to capture all atoms on the maze avoiding contact with the enemy. Once the player eat every atom the door opens and then can move to the next level.

As time passes it will go more enemies appearing each with different search patterns. Occasionally they appear eccentric enemies which have an unexpected pattern search.
